# MTRN3100 Micromouse "Manta"

UNSW Mechatronic Engineering Level 3 course MTRN3100 Robot Design group project · Team Lead

## Brief

Given the course-provided circuit board, design a robot (Micromouse) that can reliably navigate a maze.

## My Role

Mechanical design, modelling, and manufacturing; team lead. Later handled software troubleshooting and mechanical/electronic component iteration.
